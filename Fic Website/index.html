About the company

FIC was founded in May of 2013 with the goal to develop innovative software and webapplications. To push the boundaries of technology and to spread our vision.

Located in the heart of Scandinavia, Sweden, FIC operates to solve your IT related issues and launch new ideas.

Providing the service in a fast and agile way. Our expertise lies with Ruby on Rails, Python, HTML, XHTML, Javascript, JQuery, .PHP & CSS. We have clients from all over Europe. We are internationally orientated and work closely with our associates from the United States and Asia.

Through teaching we educate students in programming and business strategy, because technology binds us together to create a better world!

Ruby on Rails

Ruby on Rails is an open-source web framework that is designed and optimized for programmer happiness and maximum productivity. It lets you write beautiful code by favoring convention over configuration.

Git

Git is a free and open source distributed version control system designed to allow programmers to collaborate on small en large scaled projects.

Javascript, HTML & CSS

HTML, CSS and Javascript allow us to build, stylize and make websites interactive.
